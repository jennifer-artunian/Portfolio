# Ammonium Sulfate Production Plant – Integrated Engineering Project

## Overview

Integrated chemical engineering project for the conceptual design of an ammonium sulfate production plant.

The project includes market analysis, process and plant design, environmental impact assessment, and economic evaluation to assess the technical and financial feasibility of the proposed plant.

## Project Sections

### Market Study
Analysis of product demand, market context, and supporting visualizations.

### Plant Design
Process design documentation including engineering diagrams and plant layout.

### Environmental Analysis
Environmental impact assessment, Leopold matrix, and risk analysis.

### Economic Analysis
Economic feasibility assessment including break-even analysis, VAN, TIR, and sensitivity analysis.

## Repository Structure

- market-study → market research and supporting visualizations  
- plant-design → process diagrams, layouts, and engineering drawings  
- environmental-analysis → Leopold matrix and environmental risk analysis  
- economic-analysis → break-even, financial evaluation, and sensitivity analysis
