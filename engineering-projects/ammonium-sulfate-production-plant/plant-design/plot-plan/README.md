# Plot Plan

This section contains the spatial layout of the ammonium sulfate production facility.

Two levels of layout were developed:

## Overall Plant Layout
Shows the general site distribution within the industrial park, including:
- raw material storage tanks
- loading and unloading area
- offices and support buildings
- utilities and substation
- internal roads and safety access

## Production Area Layout
Provides a detailed view of the process area, including:
- main reactor system
- absorber
- tubular reactor and granulator
- dryers
- mixing tanks
- pumps and auxiliary equipment
- product silos and packaging area

These layouts support the conceptual plant design and ensure appropriate spatial organization of the process units and supporting infrastructure.
