# Market Study

This section contains the market analysis conducted for the ammonium sulfate production plant project.

The objective of this study was to evaluate fertilizer demand, industry trends, and market conditions to support the feasibility of the proposed production facility.

## Contents

- Fertilizer market overview
- Ammonium sulfate demand analysis
- Industry context
- Market data visualization (Tableau)

The analysis includes market research and data exploration used to justify the development of the production plant.
